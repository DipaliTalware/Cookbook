MOZZARELLA STICKS

Ingredients

  1. 1‚ÅÑ4cup flour
  2. 1 cup Italian style breadcrumbs
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 1 tablespoon milk
  5. 1 lb mozzarella cheese, cut into 3/4 inch x 3/4 inch strips (or you can use string cheese and cut each in half)
  6. 1 cup vegetable oil
  7. 1 cup jarred pizza sauce or 1 cup marinara sauce

Method:

STEP 1:
Whisk eggs and milk together.
STEP 2:
Coat each piece of cheese with flour, then dip in egg and then roll in bread crumbs.
STEP 3:
Dip in egg and crumbs again.
STEP 4:
Freeze for 6 to 24 hours before frying.
STEP 5:
Heat oil in skillet and cook sticks for about a minute on each side, or until golden and before cheese starts to leak.
STEP 6:
Drain on paper towels.
